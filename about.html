Mission Statement

At Liahona Foundation, our mission is to bridge the digital divide for students in Nigeria and the African region by providing essential laptops and tuition assistance. We aim to empower young minds to achieve their educational dreams and build a brighter future.

Vision Statement

We envision a world where every student has access to the resources they need to achieve their full potential. By providing digital tools and financial support, we aim to create a generation of educated, skilled, and confident individuals who can contribute meaningfully to their communities and the world.

History

Founded by Andrew Olsen, the Liahona Foundation emerged from a deep-seated desire to address the educational inequalities in Africa. With a background in education and a passion for technology, Andrew envisioned a foundation that could leverage digital tools to transform lives. Today, the Liahona Foundation stands as a testament to that vision, touching the lives of countless students across Nigeria.

Our Values

Compassion

We are driven by compassion to help those in need and to support the educational aspirations of students in Africa.

Innovation

We leverage technology and innovative solutions to bridge the digital divide and enhance learning opportunities.

Integrity

We uphold the highest standards of integrity and transparency in all our operations and interactions.
