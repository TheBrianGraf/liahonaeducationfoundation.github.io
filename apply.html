Apply For Our Laptop/Tuition Support

We are committed to providing essential resources to help students in Nigeria achieve their educational goals. If you are a student in need of a laptop or tuition assistance, please read the instructions below and proceed to the application.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Be a student of BYU-Pathway Worldwide (PathwayConnect or Degree) and live in Nigeria
  • Provide proof of enrollment in an educational institution
  • Submit a letter of recommendation from your local church leader (Bishop or Branch President).
Requirements

FOR YOUR APPLICATION

BYU-PathwayConnect Enrollment Verification

You must be enrolled in the BYU-PathwayConnect program and provide proof of enrollment.

Upload a 3-Minute Video About Yourself

Record a 3-minute video introducing yourself and explaining why you need our support.

Reccomendation From a Leader

Submit a letter of recommendation from your local church Leader (Bishop or Branch President).

Photo of Yourself

Upload a recent photo of yourself to help us identify you during the application process.
