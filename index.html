About Us

We Help Students In Nigeria, West Africa Region

"Education is not preparation for life; education is life itself."

John Dewey

At Liahona Education Foundation, we are dedicated to bridging the digital divide for students in the African region. By providing essential laptops and tuition assistance, we empower young minds to achieve their educational dreams and build a brighter future. Our mission is rooted in the belief that every student deserves equal access to quality education, regardless of their financial circumstances.
